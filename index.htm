Designing useful systems through research, motion, prototypes, and a little productive discomfort.

I grow the same way I design—by studying something deeply, then testing it against reality.

As a first-generation Iranian-American, I learned early how to navigate between different perspectives. Growing up between two cultures taught me to ask better questions, embrace ambiguity, and find common ground.

That curiosity has taken me from the midwest to New York, and across new communities, cultures, and problems that challenged how I think.

Principles

Study deeply · Test against reality · Keep changing perspective.
